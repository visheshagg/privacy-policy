Account and Data Deletion Instructions

These instructions refer to the CampusKart - Everything College app, developed by Vishesh Aggarwal. We are committed to respecting your privacy and providing a clear process for you to delete your account and associated personal data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

Since there is currently no automated, in-app deletion feature, you must use the following manual process to submit your request:

  • Compose an Email: Send an email from the email address associated with your CampusKart account.
  • Recipient: Address the email to our dedicated support address: visheshaggarwal1708@gmail.com.
  • Subject Line: The subject line must be: Urgent Account and Data Deletion Request.
  • Body Content: In the body of the email, clearly state your:
    • Full Name
    • CampusKart Username/Email
    • Explicit Confirmation that you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data.

Data Types Deleted and Kept

Upon receiving a valid request, Vishesh Aggarwal will process the permanent deletion of your account and most personal data within 30 days.

Category Action Details
User Account & Profile Data Deleted Your name, email, username, campus information, profile picture, and account credentials.
User Generated Content Deleted Any posts, listings, or direct messages created while using the app (Note: copies of messages sent to other users may remain on the recipient's device/account until they are also deleted).
Usage & Analytics Data Deleted/Anonymized All personal data linked to your usage (IP address, device ID) will be removed or aggregated so it can no longer identify you.
Transaction Records Retained (for a period) We may be required to keep financial transaction data (if applicable, e.g., purchase receipts) for legal or tax compliance purposes for the period required by law. This retained data will no longer be linked to your deleted user account.

Once the deletion is complete, you will receive a final confirmation email.
